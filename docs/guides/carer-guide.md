# Carer (Caregiver) User Guide

## Overview

As a Carer, you provide front-line care services. This guide covers your daily workflows using the mobile-optimized interface.

---

## Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:

- Today's scheduled shifts
- Upcoming shifts
- Pending daily reports
- Recent messages from Sponsors
- Any open escalations

---

## Check-In / Check-Out

### Starting a Shift

1. Navigate to **My Shifts**
2. Find today's shift
3. Tap **Check In**
4. Allow location access when prompted
5. Your check-in time and location are recorded

### Ending a Shift

1. Tap **Check Out** on your active shift
2. Confirm your location
3. You'll be prompted to complete your daily report

---

## Daily Reports

### Submitting a Report

1. After check-out, tap **Submit Daily Report**
2. Fill in required fields:
   - Activities performed
   - Client status observations
   - Medications administered (if applicable)
3. Add photos if needed (tap **Add Photo**)
4. Add any concerns or notes
5. Tap **Submit**

### Important Notes

- Daily report compliance affects payroll processing
- Submit reports promptly after each shift
- Photos help document care activities

---

## Escalations

### Submitting an Escalation

Use escalations for urgent issues needing immediate supervisor attention.

1. Tap **New Escalation**
2. Enter title and description
3. Select related client (if applicable)
4. Tap **Submit**
5. Your supervisor is notified immediately

### Tracking Escalations

View your escalations and their status in **My Escalations**.

---

## Chat

### Messaging Sponsors

1. Go to **Chat**
2. Select the conversation with your assigned Sponsor
3. Type your message
4. Tap **Send**

### Guidelines

- Keep communication professional
- Report any concerns through proper channels
- Chat history is retained for records

---

## Viewing Your Schedule

1. Go to **My Shifts**
2. View weekly or monthly calendar
3. Shift details show client, location, and times

---

## Payroll

View your payment history in **My Payments**:

- Completed shifts
- Payment status (Pending/Approved/Processed)
- Payment amounts
