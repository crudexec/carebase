# Sponsor User Guide

## Overview

As a Sponsor (client or family member), you can monitor care services and communicate with your assigned caregiver. This guide covers your portal access.

---

## Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:

- Assigned caregiver information
- Recent daily reports
- Unread messages
- Invoice status

---

## Daily Reports

### Viewing Reports

1. Go to **Daily Reports**
2. See list of reports from your caregiver
3. Click any report to view details

### Report Contents

Each report includes:

- Date and time of care
- Activities performed
- Client status observations
- Photos (if uploaded)
- Any notes or concerns

---

## Monthly Reports

### Viewing Monthly Summaries

1. Go to **Monthly Reports**
2. Select month to view
3. See aggregated summary of care activities

Reports are available by the 5th of each following month.

---

## Chat

### Messaging Your Caregiver

1. Go to **Chat**
2. Type your message
3. Tap **Send**

### Guidelines

- Use chat for non-urgent communication
- For emergencies, contact the care agency directly
- Chat history is saved for your records

---

## Invoices

### Viewing Invoices

1. Go to **Invoices**
2. See list of invoices with status

### Marking as Paid

1. Find the invoice
2. Click **Mark as Paid**
3. Confirm payment
4. Status updates to "Paid"

---

## Incident Reports

You will be notified of any incidents involving your care recipient after they have been reviewed and approved by the care agency.

View incident details in **Incidents** section.
